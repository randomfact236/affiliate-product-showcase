# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We aim to build a welcoming, professional community. We expect all participants to treat each other with respect.

## Expected behavior

- Use inclusive and respectful language
- Assume good intent and give constructive feedback
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Sexualized language or unwelcome advances
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or threats
- Sharing private information without permission
- Disruptive behavior that prevents collaboration

## Reporting and enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainer/owner through a private channel.

The maintainer may take actions that are appropriate to the situation, including warnings, content removal, or banning from participation.
